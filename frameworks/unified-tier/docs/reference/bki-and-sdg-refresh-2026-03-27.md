# BKI and SDG Refresh

Date: 2026-03-27
Status: draft

## Purpose

Capture two adjacent concepts that appear increasingly relevant to the Unified Tier Framework refresh:

- `BKI` = `Belonging as Knowledge Infrastructure`
- a refreshed understanding of sustainability, diversity, and governance through the SDG lens

## 1. BKI

### Working definition

`Belonging as Knowledge Infrastructure` means that belonging is not only a social good. It is part of the infrastructure that allows knowledge to be:

- shared
- trusted
- interpreted coherently
- acted on collectively

### Why it matters here

Standard wickedness frameworks focus on:

- contestation
- uncertainty
- urgency
- interdependence

But many failures in real-world change efforts also come from breakdowns in:

- trust
- representation
- interpretive alignment
- willingness to surface uncomfortable knowledge

This makes BKI relevant to:

- solution contestation
- change readiness
- coordination capability
- implementation climate

### Likely role in HUMMBL

BKI is probably not part of the core wickedness score itself.

It fits better as part of a companion layer such as:

- response readiness
- governance capacity
- implementation climate

## 2. SDG Refresh

### Sustainability

Current useful frame:

- sustainability is not only environmental stewardship
- it is integrated social, economic, and environmental viability across the UN 2030 Agenda

Primary source:

- UN 2030 Agenda: <https://sdgs.un.org/2030agenda>

### Diversity

Current useful frame:

- diversity should be treated as representation, inclusion, differential impact, and uneven access to opportunity
- it is not only a demographics category

Useful SDG anchor:

- Goal 10, Reduced Inequalities: <https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal10>

### Governance

Current useful frame:

- governance is the structure through which authority, accountability, coordination, participation, and institutional execution are organized

Useful SDG anchors:

- Goal 16, Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions: <https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal16>
- Goal 17, Partnerships for the Goals: <https://sdgs.un.org/goals/goal17>

## 3. Why This Matters For HUMMBL

The Unified Tier Framework is moving toward a more useful separation:

- structural wickedness
- response-system readiness
- implementation climate and knowledge conditions

BKI may be one of the best concepts for naming the third layer.

Possible interpretation:

- wickedness asks: how hard is the problem?
- readiness asks: can the system act?
- BKI asks: can the system know together well enough to act coherently?

## 4. Practical Use

Short-term:

- use BKI as a design note and analytic lens
- do not force it into the scalar wickedness score yet

Mid-term:

- test whether BKI-like factors explain why some similarly wicked problems have different change trajectories

## 5. Candidate BKI Dimensions

- trust
- representation quality
- interpretive alignment
- psychological safety for surfacing knowledge
- cross-boundary knowledge flow
- sense of shared stake or shared mission

## 6. Open Question

Should BKI become:

- a formal dimension family in HUMMBL
- a companion scorecard
- or a more qualitative diagnostic overlay

That should probably be decided after more real cases are scored.
